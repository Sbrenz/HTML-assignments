In The Fellowship of the Ring, Frodo Baggins inherits the One Ring, a powerful and dangerous artifact.
He sets out on a journey with a diverse group, the Fellowship, to destroy the ring in the fires of Mount Doom.
Along the way, they face challenges, encounters with different races, and ultimately, the Fellowship is divided.
Frodo and his loyal friend Sam continue the quest alone, leaving the fate of the others uncertain.
