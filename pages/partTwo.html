In The Two Towers, Frodo and Sam continue their perilous journey to destroy the One Ring.
Meanwhile, the remaining members of the Fellowship join forces with the kingdom of Rohan to face the threat of Saruman.
The story unfolds on multiple fronts, and by the end, Frodo and Sam are captured by Gollum, while the fate of Middle-earth hangs in the balance.
