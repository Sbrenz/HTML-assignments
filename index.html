The Lord of the Rings

  • The Lord of the Rings is a high fantasy novel written by J.R.R. Tolkien.
    The story is set in the fictional world of Middle-earth and follows the quest to destroy the One Ring, an evil and powerful ring created by the dark lord Sauron.
    The narrative involves various races such as hobbits, elves, dwarves, men, and other fantastical creatures.

  • The Lord of the Rings has been adapted into a highly successful film trilogy directed by Peter Jackson.

  • The trilogy consists of three books:

  1. The Fellowship of the Ring
  2. The Two Towers
  3. The Return of the King
