The Return of the King sees Frodo and Sam nearing Mount Doom to destroy the One Ring,
while the combined forces of Gondor and Rohan engage in a final battle against Sauron. Aragorn fulfills his destiny as king, leading the Free Peoples to victory. The story concludes with the destruction of the Ring,
the defeat of Sauron, and the beginning of a new era in Middle-earth.
